# Notes 6

## Question 1

### mkdir
#### Definition: 
Creates a new directory (folder).
#### Usage/Formula:
`mkdir directory_name`
#### Examples:
`mkdir projects`
`mkdir homework`


## Question 2
### touch
#### Definition:
Creates a new empty file.
#### Usage/Formula:
`touch filename`
#### Examples:
`touch notes.txt`
`touch index.html`


## Question 3
### rm
#### Definition: 
Removes (deletes) files or directories.
#### Usage/Formula:
`rm filename`
`rm -r directory_name`
#### Examples:
`rm notes.txt`
`rm -r old_folder`

## Question 4
### cp
#### Definition: 
Copies files or directories from one location to another.
#### Usage/Formula:
`cp source destination`
`cp -r source_directory destination`
#### Examples:
`cp notes.txt backup.txt`
`cp -r project project_copy`

## Question 5
### mv
#### Definition:
Moves or renames files and directories.
#### Usage/Formula:
`mv source destination`
#### Examples:
`mv notes.txt documents/`
`mv oldname.txt newname.txt`
